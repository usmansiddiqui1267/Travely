Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TravelBudget

How do I create my first trip? +

To create your first trip, click on "Create Trip" from your dashboard or the trips page. You'll need to provide a trip name, destination, dates, and initial budget. Once created, you can start adding expenses and itinerary items.

Is my financial data secure? +

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your financial data is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers. For added security, we recommend using a secure device and keeping your login credentials private.

Can I use TravelBudget on multiple devices? +

Currently, TravelBudget stores data locally in your browser, so your data is tied to the device you're using. We're working on cloud synchronization features that will allow you to access your trips across multiple devices.

How do I export my expense reports? +

You can export your expense reports by going to the Reports page and clicking the "Download PDF" button. This will generate a comprehensive report of your expenses that you can save or print for your records.

Is TravelBudget completely free? +

Yes, TravelBudget is completely free to use with all core features available at no cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the basic trip planning and expense tracking will always remain free.
